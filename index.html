Spatialspec

Spatial specification and solutions for all.

Feature: Capture of site features including buildings, fences, trees, and services.

Contour: Ground levels and contour mapping for design and drainage analysis.

Volume: Stockpile and excavation volume calculations for earthworks management.

Topographic: Comprehensive terrain mapping combining features, levels, and landforms.

Elevation: Building and floor level surveys for construction verification and compliance.

Setout: Precise transfer of design coordinates to the field for accurate positioning of structures.

As-con: Documentation of completed works to verify compliance with specifications.

Drone: Aerial surveys for large-scale mapping, progress monitoring, and site documentation.

Scan: 3D laser scanning for detailed capture of complex structures and environments.

Monitor: Periodic surveys to track movement, settlement, or deformation over time.

General: CAD drafting services to produce plans, sections, and detailed drawings.

Survey: Production of survey plans from field data for design and documentation.

Data: Processing and organising survey data into usable formats and deliverables.

Report: Technical reports and documentation for project requirements and compliance.

Model: 3D modelling for visualisation, design review, and project coordination.
