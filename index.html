Spatialspec

Spatial specification and solutions for all.

Feature: Comprehensive capture of all site features including buildings, fences, trees, retaining walls, and underground services. Our feature surveys provide accurate spatial data essential for architectural design, engineering planning, and property development. We locate and map all visible improvements and infrastructure to create a complete picture of existing conditions.
Contour: Precise ground level surveys and contour mapping for design development and drainage analysis. Contour surveys establish the natural topography of a site, enabling engineers and architects to design with the land. Essential for stormwater management, cut and fill calculations, and ensuring compliant building platforms.
Volume: Accurate stockpile and excavation volume calculations for effective earthworks management. We provide before and after surveys to quantify material movements, verify contractor claims, and maintain inventory records. Ideal for mining operations, construction sites, and quarry management.
Elevation: Building and floor level surveys for construction verification and regulatory compliance. We establish vertical datums, verify floor levels against approved plans, and provide certification for building surveyors. Critical for multi-storey construction, flood-prone areas, and council compliance requirements.
Construction setout: Precise transfer of design coordinates from plans to the physical site for accurate positioning of structures and infrastructure. Our setout services ensure that buildings, roads, pipelines, and civil works are constructed exactly as designed. We work directly with contractors to establish reference points, grid lines, and level controls throughout the construction process.
As-constructed: Comprehensive documentation of completed works to verify compliance with approved designs and specifications. As-constructed surveys capture the final positions of all built elements, providing essential records for asset management, future maintenance, and regulatory certification. Required for infrastructure handover and council acceptance.
Monitoring: Systematic periodic surveys to track structural movement, ground settlement, or deformation over time. Monitoring programs provide early warning of potential issues, verify design assumptions, and satisfy regulatory requirements. Essential for excavations near existing structures, retaining walls, and sensitive infrastructure.
General: Professional CAD drafting services to produce plans, sections, and detailed drawings for construction and development projects. We work with architects, engineers, and builders to translate concepts into technical documentation. Our drawings meet industry standards and council submission requirements for residential, commercial, and industrial projects.
Data: Processing and organising survey data into usable formats and deliverables tailored to client requirements. We convert raw field data into CAD files, GIS layers, and digital terrain models. Our data processing ensures accuracy, consistency, and compatibility with your existing systems and workflows.
Report: Technical reports and documentation for project requirements, regulatory compliance, and stakeholder communication. We prepare survey reports, certificates, and supporting documentation for development applications, construction certifications, and asset records. Clear, professional reports that stand up to scrutiny.
Model: 3D modelling for visualisation, design review, and project coordination across disciplines. We create detailed digital models from survey data for BIM integration, clash detection, and client presentations. Our models help stakeholders understand spatial relationships and make informed decisions before construction begins.
Support: Technical support services for survey equipment, software, and workflow optimisation. We provide guidance on instrument setup, data collection procedures, and troubleshooting common issues. Our support helps teams maintain productivity and achieve accurate results in the field and office.
