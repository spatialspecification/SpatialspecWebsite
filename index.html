Spatialspec

Spatial specification and technical solutions.

Feature: Accurate capture of buildings, utilities, and natural features—essential data for informed planning and design decisions.

Contour: Precise ground level mapping and contour analysis to optimise drainage design, site grading, and earthworks planning.

Volume: Reliable stockpile and excavation calculations that help you track quantities, manage costs, and verify earthworks progress.

Topographic: Complete terrain intelligence combining features, elevations, and landforms—the foundation for every successful project.

Elevation: Certified floor and building level surveys for construction verification, compliance certificates, and quality assurance.

Setout: Millimetre-precision positioning of design elements on site—ensuring your structures are built exactly where they should be.

As-built: Comprehensive documentation of completed works for compliance verification, handover packages, and permanent records.

Drone: Rapid aerial surveys delivering high-resolution orthophotos, progress tracking, and large-scale site documentation in hours, not days.

Scan: High-definition 3D laser scanning for complex structures, heritage buildings, and environments where detail matters most.

Monitor: Ongoing structural and ground movement monitoring to detect changes early and protect your assets over time.

CAD: Professional drafting of plans, sections, and detailed drawings that communicate your project clearly to all stakeholders.

Survey Plans: Publication-ready survey plans transformed from raw field data into polished, client-ready deliverables.

Data: Expert processing and conversion of survey data into formats that integrate seamlessly with your design software.

Reports: Clear, professional technical documentation that meets regulatory requirements and supports project approvals.

Modelling: Detailed 3D models that bring your projects to life—enabling better design review, clash detection, and stakeholder communication.

Support: Expert troubleshooting for survey equipment, software, and field workflows—minimising downtime and keeping your team productive.

Training: Tailored mentoring that accelerates staff development, builds technical confidence, and creates clear pathways to career goals.

Optimisation: Strategic analysis of your workflows, systems, and technology stack to eliminate inefficiencies and reduce operational costs.
