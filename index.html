Spatialspec

Spatial specification and technical solutions.

Feature: Comprehensive site documentation capturing buildings, structures, utilities, vegetation, and all physical elements of your property. We use total stations and GNSS technology to deliver accurate coordinates and heights for every feature. Ideal for architects, engineers, and planners who need reliable base data before commencing design work.

Contour: Detailed ground surface modelling that reveals how water flows across your site and where levels change. We capture spot heights at strategic intervals and generate contour lines at your required spacing. Essential for drainage design, cut-and-fill calculations, and understanding site constraints before construction begins.

Volume: Precise measurement of stockpiles, excavations, and material quantities using survey-grade equipment. We provide before-and-after comparisons, progress volumes, and certified quantity reports. Perfect for quarries, construction sites, and any project requiring accurate material tracking for payment or inventory purposes.

Topographic: The complete picture—combining features, contours, and site boundaries into one comprehensive survey deliverable. We capture everything visible on site and present it in CAD-ready formats compatible with all major design software. The foundation document that architects, engineers, and planners rely on throughout the project lifecycle.

Elevation: Precise floor level and building height surveys for construction compliance and certification. We verify that slabs, floors, and structures meet design specifications and council requirements. Delivered with formal reports suitable for building surveyors, certifiers, and regulatory submissions.

Setout: Transferring design coordinates from drawings to the physical site with millimetre precision. We mark positions for footings, columns, walls, services, and any element requiring accurate placement. Using robotic total stations and GNSS, we ensure your construction team builds exactly where the design intended—avoiding costly rework and delays.

As-built: Documenting what was actually constructed versus what was designed. We survey completed structures, underground services, and finished works to create permanent records. Deliverables include certified plans, 3D models, and reports suitable for handover documentation, asset management systems, and future reference.

Drone: Aerial surveying and photogrammetry capturing large areas quickly and safely. We deliver orthomosaic imagery, digital terrain models, and volumetric data from above. Ideal for progress documentation, stockpile measurement, site planning, and capturing areas difficult to access on foot.

Scan: High-definition 3D laser scanning that captures millions of points per second, creating precise digital replicas of existing conditions. We scan buildings, industrial facilities, heritage structures, and complex environments. The resulting point clouds integrate directly into BIM software for renovation planning, clash detection, and detailed analysis.

Monitor: Scheduled surveys tracking movement, settlement, and deformation over time. We establish baseline measurements and return at agreed intervals to detect any changes. Early warning data helps protect structures, verify construction impacts on neighbours, and satisfy regulatory monitoring requirements.

CAD: Professional drafting services producing plans, sections, elevations, and detailed construction drawings. We work in AutoCAD, Civil 3D, and other industry-standard platforms to deliver files that integrate with your existing workflows. Whether you need drawings from scratch or existing plans refined, we deliver presentation-quality results.

Survey Plans: Transforming raw field measurements into polished, client-ready survey plans. We apply proper symbology, annotations, and formatting that meets industry standards. Final deliverables are suitable for council submissions, client presentations, and permanent project records.

Data: Processing and converting survey data into formats your design team can use immediately. We handle coordinate transformations, file conversions, and data cleanup to ensure seamless integration with Civil 3D, 12d, Revit, and other platforms. No more struggling with incompatible files or manual data entry.

Reports: Professional technical documentation including survey reports, methodology statements, and compliance certificates. We prepare clear, well-structured documents that satisfy regulatory requirements and support planning applications. Every report is reviewed for accuracy and presented to a professional standard.

Modelling: Creating detailed 3D models from survey data for visualisation, design coordination, and stakeholder communication. We build terrain models, building information models, and existing conditions models that integrate with BIM workflows. See your project in three dimensions before construction begins—identifying issues early when changes cost less.

Support: On-call technical assistance for survey equipment, software, and field procedures. When your total station won't connect, your software throws errors, or your team encounters an unfamiliar situation, we provide rapid troubleshooting to get you back on track. Minimise costly downtime and keep your projects moving forward.

Training: Personalised mentoring and skills development for survey staff at all experience levels. We work with individuals to identify career goals, strengthen technical capabilities, and develop confidence in the field and office. From graduate surveyors finding their feet to experienced staff adopting new technologies—we help your people grow.

Optimisation: Comprehensive review of your survey operations to identify where time and money are being lost. We analyse workflows, evaluate hardware and software choices, and recommend practical improvements. From field procedures to office processing, we help you work smarter—delivering more projects with the same resources.
